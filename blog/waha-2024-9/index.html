Session Metadata

metadata is an attribute on Session objects that lets you store more information, structured as key-value pairs, to sessions for your own use and reference. For example, you can store your user‚Äôs unique identifier from your system.

Associated metadata field is available in:

  1. List Sessions and Get Session responses
  2. üîÑ Webhooks events
  3. üìä Dashboard for view, and search sessions by metadata

Sample metadata use cases:

  • Link IDs: Attach your system‚Äôs unique IDs to a Session object to simplify lookups. For example, add your user or tenant id.
  • Customer details: Annotate a customer by storing an internal information (email, customer name) for your future use, so you don’t have to look into two systems.

WAHA does not use metadata for any internal purposes, it’s up to you how to use it.
