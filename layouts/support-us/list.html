WAHA (WhatsApp HTTP API) is 100% free and open source.

All features are available for everyone - unlimited sessions, media messages, all storages, built-in security, and more. There's no separate "Plus" image anymore.

WAHA is built and maintained by a small team. Everything is free, but if it helps you, you can support the project by subscribing to the Community tier on one of the platforms: Boosty, Patreon or using Crypto.

WAHA 🤔 Others
